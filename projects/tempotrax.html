Thank you for checking out my project! This application will create a Spotify playlist that consists of songs from your liked Spotify songs. Use the sliders to set 'danceablity', 'energy' and min/max 'tempo' parameters and the playlist will dinamically adjust to the settings. Once you find a playlist you like, click 'generate playlist' and the playlist will be sent to your Spotify account. You will be prompted for a title for your new playlist, if one is not provided TempoTrax will give a default name.

Be aware that I make no promises this will work on mobile devices, in fact, I am certain features will not behave correctly on mobile devices, for best experience, please use a desktop computer with a modern browser.
